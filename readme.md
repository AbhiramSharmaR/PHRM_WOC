AI-Powered Unified Healthcare Management Platform

Healthcare data fragmentation remains a major barrier to effective patient care, clinical efficiency, and family involvement. This project addresses these challenges by delivering a secure, AI-powered healthcare management platform that unifies patients, doctors, and family members within a single digital ecosystem.

The platform centralizes health information, enables real-time monitoring, and provides intelligent decision support to improve communication, continuity of care, and healthcare accessibility.
